Portfolio Development

Portfolio preparation for high school and college bound students. We can help you develop a great portfolio with a variety of mediums, techniques, and styles.

Whatever your interest, we’ll work with you, one on one, to create a great portfolio that meets school guidelines for content and presentation and prepare you for the audition tests.
